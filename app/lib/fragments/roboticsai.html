Grade-wise progression

The right challenge for every age group

The curriculum is structured across three stages, so a Primary student and a Senior student each get work that's exciting and achievable for them.

Primary (Grades 1–5)

Spark & explore

Playful first steps — simple Micro:bit animations, basic sensors, and friendly builds that turn curiosity into confidence. Students discover that technology is something they can make.

Intermediate (Grades 6–8)

Build & control

Real robotics begins — coding microcontrollers, wiring motors and sensors, and building machines like line-following and obstacle-avoiding robots that act on their own logic.

Senior (Grades 9–10)

Automate & innovate

Advanced projects bring AI and automation together — IoT dashboards, computer vision, and intelligent robotic systems that solve genuine real-world problems.
