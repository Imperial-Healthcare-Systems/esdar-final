About Esdar

Technology, taught by building it

Who We Are

ESDAR is an innovation-driven education initiative dedicated to empowering students with practical knowledge in Robotics and Artificial Intelligence.

What We Do

We specialize in creating engaging, hands-on learning experiences that allow students to explore technology beyond textbooks.

How We Teach

Our approach emphasizes learning by doing, where students actively build, experiment, and understand concepts in a structured and guided environment.

Our Mission

Through our programs and workshops, we nurture curiosity, creativity, and problem-solving skills to prepare students for future technology opportunities.

Aim & Vision

Building a generation of creators

At Esdar, our vision is to build a generation of students who are well aware of Robotics & AI — innovators, problem-solvers, and creators of technology, capable of applying their knowledge to real-world challenges.
